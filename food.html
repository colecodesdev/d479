Grocery Stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Taniti Market

A full-service supermarket

Oceanview Grocers

Large selection of groceries

Palm Leaf Foods

Small neighborhood grocery store offering everyday essentials

Sunrise Provisions

Cozy local shop with a great selection of groceries & snacks

Night Owl Express

24-hour convenience store
